Welcome to Ilam

Queen of the Hills - Discover the serenity of tea gardens and Himalayan vistas.

Top Attractions in Ilam

Antu Danda

Marvel at stunning sunrise views over the Himalayas, surrounded by lush tea gardens.

Kanyam Tea Garden

Explore vibrant tea estates with scenic trails, perfect for a refreshing escape.

Mai Pokhari

Visit the sacred lake nestled in serene forests, a haven for nature and spirituality.

Local Delights

Tongba

Warm millet-based drink, a cozy local favorite.

Sukuti

Spicy dried meat, perfect for festive evenings.

Gundruk & Sinki

Fermented veggies in flavorful soups or curries.

How to Reach Ilam

By Bus

Night bus from Kathmandu to Birtamod, then jeep to Ilam.

By Air

Fly to Bhadrapur from Kathmandu, then a 2-hour drive.

From India

Enter via Kakarbhitta from Darjeeling or Siliguri.
